POLS Aggie Journal on the United States, China, and the World Order

POLS Aggie Journal on the United States, China, and the World Order showcases undergraduate research on the dynamic relationship between the United States, China, and the global order. The journal is overseen by Qiang (Steven) Wu, the instructor of the course that explores these critical topics.
