Qiang (Steven) Wu

Hello, and welcome to my website! I'm Qiang (Steven) Wu, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Political Science at the Bush School of Government & Public Service, Texas A&M University. My academic journey delves into the relationship between international political economy and international security, with an emphasis on U.S.-China Relations. Additionally, I investigate the nexus between democratization and trade liberalization, as well as public opinion concerning trade policy and globalization.
I've gained professional experience in China, including roles at the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National People’s Congress and at Peking University. Prior to joining Texas A&M, I earned an M.A. in International Economics and China Studies from Johns Hopkins University’s School of Advanced International Studies, with a specialization in Economic Policy. My educational background also includes an M.A. in Economics from Kyoto University, as well as an M.A. in International Relations and a B.A. in Political Science from Peking University.
